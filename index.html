I2C Dataset

Introduction

1. What's I2C dataset?

I2C dataset is a dataset of images and their associated comments from Flickr, which have been well post-processed to support the research in image commenting area.

2. What's image commenting?

Automatically expressing opinions on an image, which is referred to as image commenting. Since image is becoming a predominant medium for social interactions, image commenting has great potential to improve user engagement and thus becomes an emerging yet very challenging research topic.

3. Why I2C dataset?

I2C dataset is a large dataset containing 426K images and their associated comments from Flickr, which makes it useful and suitable in image commenting area. Images in this dataset are crawled using about 800 popular tags, covering a wide range of topics such as animal(e.g., bird, cat), nature(e.g., beach, sunrise) and sports(e.g., surfing, swimming) in daily life and becoming diverse enough to train a practical model.

Statistics

1. Overall:

  • Number of images: 426K
  • Number of associated comments: 11M
  • Number of comments per image on average: 25
  • Number of topics covered: 13
