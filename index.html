I2C Dataset

Welcome to the page for I2C dataset

I2C dataset is a dataset with 426K images and 11M associated and well-processed comments from Flickr to support the research in image commenting area. The dataset covers a wide range of topics such as animal(e.g., bird, cat), nature(e.g., beach, sunrise) and sports(e.g., surfing, swimming). The distribution of topics is presented below:

  More information about images like tags, user descriptions and location are also available.
